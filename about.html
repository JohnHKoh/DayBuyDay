Our Mission

Here at Day-Buy-Day, our mission is to empower members so that they may purchase and commemorate a day for a special person that you may have in mind. Our slogan "Your own day. Forever." comes from the fact that we understand the importance of a day. With our special program, members can gift their special days and dedicate them to others as a gift.

Chris Raley, CEO

"Our mission is to serve you and your needs for any day."

In order to provide the stepping stones to finding that perfect gift for that special someone, we have given customers full control over what day they would like to purchase. By purchasing a specific day, we will deliver a unique certificate just for you so that you may dedicate that day to any sort of specific event, person, or object of interest! If you're looking for a special gift that won't be forgettable, Day-Buy-Day is your final destination.

What sets us apart from our competitors is allowing users to pick and choose a specific day in the year to own. Once the user has owned the day, they may choose to dedicate this day in order to celebrate or dedicate a specific event or person. By unleashing this opportunity to our customers we hope to see the satisfaction that comes from giving a perfect gift.
