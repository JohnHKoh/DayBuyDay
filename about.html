Our Mission

Here at Day-Buy-Day our mission is to empower users so that they may purchase and commemorate a special day for a special person that you may have in mind. It's our responsibility to competently revolutionize client-focused services so that we may strive for 100% customer satisfaction. Our slogan "Our days our limited. Come buy one" comes from the fact that we know the importance of a day. We wanted to record 9/11 forever... never forget so we made a website to do that.

In order to provide the stepping stones to finding that perfect gift for that special someone, we have given customers full control over what day they would like to purchase. By purchasing a specific day, we will deliver a unique certificate just for you so that you may dedicate that day to any sort of specific event, person, or object of interest! If you're looking for a special gift that won't be forgettable, we recommend buying a day.

What sets us apart from our competitors is allowing users to pick and choose a specific day in the year to own. Once the user has owned the day, they may choose to dedicate this day in order to celebrate or dedicate a specific event or person. Too many times have we experienced the need to get a special gift for someone special. We wish to change this narrative and allow users to own a specific day and dedicate it to someone else as a gift. By unleashing this opportunity to our customers we hope to see the satisfaction that comes from giving a perfect gift.
